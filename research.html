Research Projects

Master's Thesis Project

Title: Vaccine Hesitancy, Social Media Usage, Institutional Trust, and Politicization

This project led to the co-writing of a publication in collaboration with Jeremy Ward (sociologist) and Christopher McKinley (health communication researcher). We investigated how social media use interacts with institutional trust to shape vaccine hesitancy.

Publication:

  • McKinley CJ, Olivier E, Ward JK. The Influence of Social Media and Institutional Trust on Vaccine Hesitancy in France. Vaccines. 2023; 11(8):1319. Read article

Main tasks:

  • Scientific writing
  • Protocol design
  • Statistical analyses (descriptive analysis, mediation/moderation models, logistic regression)

Software used: R, Python

Institutions: CNRS (UMR 8211), Inserm (U988)

PhD Thesis Project

Title: Glucocorticoid Use and Cancer Risk in Large Real-World Healthcare Databases

As a PhD student in pharmacoepidemiology, I focus on the potential long-term cancer risks associated with glucocorticoid use in large real-world populations.

Sub-projects:

  • Skin cancer risk among women using topical and systemic glucocorticoids
  • Systematic review of methodological challenges in observational cancer risk studies
  • Emulation of target trials using national administrative healthcare data
