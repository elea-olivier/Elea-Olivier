Sorbonne Nord University

Semester 4 - Introduction to Demography and Statistics

  • Role: Tutorial Instructor.
  • Audience: 2nd year students in Health and Social Sciences (SSS).
  • Teaching hours: 21 hours.
  • Schedule: Every Monday afternoon, from January 28 to March 11 (7 weeks).
  • Program page: Licence SSS .

Learning Objectives

This course aims to introduce students to the fundamentals of demography and statistics. By the end of the sessions, they will be able to:

  • Define key concepts in demography and descriptive statistics.
  • Calculate indicators such as standard deviation, variance, and correlation coefficient.
  • Interpret graphs and tables from scientific publications.
  • Describe statistical techniques used in biomedical research.
